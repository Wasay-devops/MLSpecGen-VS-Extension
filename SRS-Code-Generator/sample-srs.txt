Software Requirements Specification (SRS)
Sample E-Commerce System

1. FUNCTIONALITY: User Authentication
   Description: The system shall provide user authentication functionality to allow users to log in and log out securely.
   Context: This functionality is essential for maintaining user sessions and securing user data.
   Requirements:
   - The system shall validate user credentials against stored user data
   - The system shall provide secure password hashing
   - The system shall maintain user sessions
   - The system shall handle login failures gracefully

2. FUNCTIONALITY: Product Catalog Management
   Description: The system shall allow administrators to manage product catalogs including adding, updating, and removing products.
   Context: This functionality enables store administrators to maintain the product inventory and information.
   Requirements:
   - The system shall allow adding new products with name, description, price, and category
   - The system shall allow updating existing product information
   - The system shall allow removing products from the catalog
   - The system shall validate product data before saving

3. FUNCTIONALITY: Shopping Cart
   Description: The system shall provide shopping cart functionality for users to add, remove, and modify items before checkout.
   Context: This functionality allows users to collect items they want to purchase and manage their order.
   Requirements:
   - The system shall allow adding items to the cart
   - The system shall allow removing items from the cart
   - The system shall allow updating item quantities
   - The system shall calculate total cart value
   - The system shall persist cart data during user session

4. FUNCTIONALITY: Order Processing
   Description: The system shall process customer orders including payment validation and order confirmation.
   Context: This functionality handles the final step of the purchase process.
   Requirements:
   - The system shall validate payment information
   - The system shall process payment transactions
   - The system shall generate order confirmations
   - The system shall update inventory after successful orders
   - The system shall send confirmation emails to customers

5. FUNCTIONALITY: Inventory Management
   Description: The system shall track product inventory levels and provide low-stock alerts.
   Context: This functionality helps maintain adequate stock levels and prevent stockouts.
   Requirements:
   - The system shall track current inventory levels
   - The system shall update inventory when orders are processed
   - The system shall generate low-stock alerts
   - The system shall prevent overselling of out-of-stock items
